Work in Bulgaria

Top 10 Reasons to Work in Bulgaria

  • 1

    Emerging Tech Hub

    Bulgaria is rapidly becoming a major center for IT and software development in Eastern Europe, with many multinational offices.

  • 2

    Extremely Low living Costs

    Bulgaria offers one of the lowest costs of living in the entire European Union, making your salary go much further.

  • 3

    Competitive Tax Rates

    The country has some of the most favorable corporate and personal income tax rates in Europe.

  • 4

    EU Membership Benefits

    Working in Bulgaria gives you the rights and mobility that come with residing in the European Union.

  • 5

    Fastest Internet in Europe

    Bulgaria boasts some of the world's fastest and most reliable broadband internet, perfect for digital roles.

  • 6

    Multilingual Environment

    English is widely spoken in the professional community, making it easy for international workers to integrate.

  • 7

    Safe and Stable Living

    Bulgaria is a peaceful country with a growing middle class and a safe social environment.

  • 8

    Beautiful Nature

    From the Black Sea coast to stunning mountain ranges, Bulgaria offers amazing weekend getaways.

  • 9

    Rich Cultural History

    Experience a unique blend of ancient Thracian, Roman, and Ottoman influences in daily life.

  • 10

    Career Growth Opportunities

    With many startups and expanding international companies, Bulgaria offers rapid career advancement for talented workers.

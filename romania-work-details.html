Work in Romania

Top 10 Reasons to Work in Romania

  • 1

    Fastest Economic Growth

    Romania has one of the highest economic growth rates in Eastern Europe, creating a wealth of new job opportunities.

  • 2

    Booming IT Sector

    Romania is a global hub for software development and IT outsourcing, with many multinational tech giants operating there.

  • 3

    Ultra-Fast Internet

    Romania consistently ranks among the top countries for high-speed internet, ideal for tech and remote work roles.

  • 4

    Low Cost of Living

    Enjoy a very high standard of living with significantly lower costs for housing, food, and utilities compared to Western Europe.

  • 5

    EU Membership Benefits

    Working in Romania grants you the rights, mobility, and social protections of a European Union resident.

  • 6

    Safety and Security

    Romania is one of the safest countries in Europe, with low crime rates and a welcoming social environment.

  • 7

    Diverse Career Paths

    Opportunities abound not only in IT but also in manufacturing, services, and the creative industries.

  • 8

    Stunning Natural Landscapes

    From the Carpathian Mountains to the Black Sea coast, Romania offers beautiful natural sites to explore during your time off.

  • 9

    Rich Cultural Scene

    Experience a vibrant blend of historical charm and modern European culture in cities like Bucharest and Cluj.

  • 10

    Competitive Salaries

    Salaries in Romania are growing rapidly, providing great local purchasing power and financial stability.
